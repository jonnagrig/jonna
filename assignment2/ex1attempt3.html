PERFECT BORSCHT

Serves 4

Back to Assignment Page







Cut ¾ of the  300G PEELED BEETROOT     into small dice (you may want to wear rubber gloves to do this) and set the rest aside. Melt the  50G OF BUTTER  in a large pan, and then soften the  1 SMALL ONION  over a gentle heat for 5 minutes.

Add the  1 SMALL CARROT, 1 SMALL LEEK (all peeled where necessary and cut into small dice or rings), 1 SMALL STICK OF CELERY,  225g of diced beetroot,  2 GRAINS OF ALLSPICE and ½ BAY LEAF  and stir well to coat with butter. Cook for another 10 minutes, adding a little stock if the vegetables begin to look dry. Meanwhile, grate the remaining 75g of beetroot.

Pour in the rest of the  1.5L OF GELATINOUS BEEF STOCK  and the  2 PEELED AND DICED MEDIUM FLOURY POTATOES (eg Maris Piper)  and simmer for 15 minutes, then add the  ½ SMALL SHREDDED CABBAGE, 4 PEELED AND CRUSHED CLOVES OF GARLIC  and grated beetroot. Cook until all the vegetables are tender (about 10 minutes).

Add the  2 TBSP OF CIDER VINEGAR, 1 TSP OF SUGAR, ½ TSP GROUND BLACK PEPPER and a PINCH OF SALT  and taste. Add a little more of any of these if necessary, then serve with a < em>dollop of SOUR CREAM, a sprig of DILL, and some Polish bread  on the side.
